Seller In

Place

  • A Seller in Place (SIP) is also known as a Contingency
  • Contingencies can be used for a number of things in a contract when you buy or sell
  • A contingency basically means "I will buy/sell this property contingent on..."
  • For example, an SIP would read "I will sell my current property contingent on closing escrow for my new home"
  • This allows you to lock in your new dream home without multiple moves or any risk

An SIP is just 1 strategic tool I use to simplify your homebuying experience
